Our Comprehensive Mission

The California Rail Foundation (CRF) stands at the intersection of environmental sustainability and technical progress. Our mission is to accelerate the transition to a modernized, high-speed rail network that serves as the backbone of California's economy.

We believe that rail is more than just transportation; it is a catalyst for social equity. By connecting major urban hubs with underserved rural areas, we create pathways for jobs, education, and healthcare. Our foundation works tirelessly to ensure that every rail dollar is spent with transparency and foresight.

Since our inception in Sacramento, we have focused on reducing the state's carbon footprint. By advocating for electrified rail systems, we aim to eliminate thousands of tons of greenhouse gases annually, making California a global leader in green transit innovation.

Our commitment extends to the next generation. Through our technical mentorship programs, we are training the engineers and planners who will build the rail corridors of the 2030s and beyond.

"The future of California is on tracks. Efficient rail is the only way to meet our climate goals while supporting a growing population."

— Board of Directors, CRF

Strategic Initiatives

Our work spans from technical corridor analysis to public policy advocacy.

High-Speed Rail Integration

We lead technical task forces that study the integration of regional rail with the future California High-Speed Rail network, ensuring seamless transfers for millions of passengers.

Technical Research Hub

Our foundation publishes annual "State of the Rail" reports that provide non-partisan data on safety, efficiency, and environmental impact of transit projects.

Transit-Oriented Housing

Advocating for smart urban growth. We support policies that build affordable housing near rail stations to reduce car dependency and improve quality of life.

Privacy & Data Ethics

California Rail Foundation (CRF) respects the privacy of all donors and partners. We strictly adhere to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and ensure that no personal data is sold or shared. Our data collection is limited to essential communications regarding our non-profit mission.

Legal Transparency

As a registered 501(c)(3) entity (EIN 68-0131222), CRF is committed to absolute transparency. All financial reports and project evaluations are filed according to federal and state regulations. Our resources are dedicated 100% to charitable and educational rail advocacy.
