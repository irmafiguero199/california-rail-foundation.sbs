Strategic Mission & Objectives

The California Rail Foundation serves as a vital resource for public education, historical preservation, and sustainable infrastructure research. Founded on the principle that rail is a cornerstone of economic growth, our foundation works tirelessly to document the history of the California rail system while providing data-driven advocacy for its modernization.

Our work encompasses the digital archiving of rare historical blueprints, the restoration of heritage rail assets, and the publication of comprehensive reports that inform state policy. We believe in transparency, public service, and the preservation of the engineering marvels that built the Golden State.
